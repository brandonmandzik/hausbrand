Groovery

Sun, Jul 26, 2020 One-minute read

The summer strives and it’s hot in berlin. Practically it’s a hard corona lockdown for everybody. But well … the berliner club community is fighting hard for finding solutions. I got the chance to party a bit at limited open airs. 200 people max. Hm somehow it was still fine because it was better than nothing right. Party people even throw illegal raves at some point … scary in these times. Grooving through summer I recorded my second set to catch my summer feeling. I called it Groovery because “Welocme to the Groovery Store”.
